Recommendations for quoting, linking and compiling a bibliography of scientific texts (auto-translate)

Recommendations are made for the design of footnotes and bibliographic lists in accordance with the requirements of GOST R 7.0.5 -2008 "Bibliography link. General requirements and rules for compiling " and GOST 7.1-2003" Bibliographic record. Bibliographic description. General requirements and drafting rules ". These rules are recommended for use in the design of scientific materials in the Russian scientific literature and periodicals. For bibliographic apparatus in foreign publications, other approaches to citing are used, for example, Harvard system .

Basic elements of bibliographic description

In general, the sequence of bibliographic description elements is as follows:

  • additional title of the publication (if the work is part of a composite edition);
  •  / [one slash] statement of responsibility; for example, an indication of responsible editors, compilers, other authors, and t. d. (optional);
  • . - [dot, space, dash] city of publication;
  • : [colon] publisher;
  • , [comma] year of publication;
  • . - [dot, dash] number of pages (for a book as a whole in the bibliographic description: "295 p."; for a part of the book in a bibliographic description or in a subscript link to individual pages: "S. C. 201–236 ";" C. 56 etc.).
